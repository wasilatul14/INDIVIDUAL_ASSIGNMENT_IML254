University

I was allowed to enroll in UiTM Merbok, Kedah for two and a half years at the diploma level in library informatics on October 10, 2022. Since I had never lived in a dorm while attending school, I was first terrified to live in college. Another fear I have is not being able to adjust to a new environment. However, I am appreciative of my supportive roommates and classmates. I saw a variety of people with a range of personalities in the four semesters I was here. Furthermore, I have the ability to acquire several accents from various nations. My time here will end next year, so I really hope we stay in contact and get to meet each other.
